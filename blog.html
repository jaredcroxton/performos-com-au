About this blog

The PerformOS Studio blog is part of the AI Fluency resources, a growing set of guides for Australian small business operators who want to work with AI, not just read about it.

Articles are published as they ship. No content calendar filler. No SEO-for-its-own-sake. Every piece answers a real question an owner has asked us, or addresses a friction point we have seen in the field.

What we write about

  • AI instruments. Which ones work, which ones do not, and how to set them up.
  • Adoption strategy. How to bring AI into a small business without breaking what already works.
  • Operator technique. Prompt patterns, workflow design, and the craft of working alongside AI.
  • Australian context. Local pricing, local regulation, local case studies.

What we avoid

  • Generic AI explainers that read like a Wikipedia article.
  • Vendor press releases dressed as reviews.
  • Articles that end with "but it depends" and no clear recommendation.
