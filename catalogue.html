PulseCheck 360

Flight-risk detection for frontline teams. A three-minute weekly check-in that turns team sentiment into organisational intelligence. Detects flight risk before it becomes attrition. Generates draft one-on-one prompts so the manager walks into the conversation already informed.

What it removes

The moment a good team member resigns and the manager realises they never saw it coming. The gap between how someone is actually doing and what the manager assumes.

Who it is for

Team leads, HR business partners, and ops directors responsible for retention in high-turnover environments.

How it works

  • Team members complete a three-minute weekly check-in
  • PulseCheck 360 reads every response and tracks sentiment over time
  • Managers get flagged when a team member moves from healthy to at risk to flight risk
  • AI-generated one-on-one prompts arrive before the conversation, not after
