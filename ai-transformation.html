How we work

Five steps. Fixed price. Real results.

01

We assess

One conversation. We look at your operation: what tasks consume the most time, where decisions get stuck, what data sits in five different places. We map the friction points you already feel but have not named. No consulting deck. A one-page friction map you can read in five minutes.

02

We design

We design the agent architecture. Which tasks go to AI. Which stay with people. Where the handoff happens. How the workflows connect. We design for safety: clear boundaries, human review points, outputs you can inspect before they ship. No black boxes. No "trust the algorithm."

03

We train

Your team needs to know how to work alongside AI. Not how to code. How to prompt. How to review. How to override. When to trust the output and when to challenge it. We run the AI Fluency Workshop -- half-day, on-site or remote -- and your team leaves with a working instrument configured for your actual business.

04

We build

Four-week implementation sprint. Fixed scope. Fixed price. We build the agents, configure the workflows, connect the instruments, and ship them into production. Your team is using AI on real work by the end of week four. Not piloting. Not testing. Shipping.

05

We stay

Most consultants leave when the project ends. We stay. Your instruments are owned, not rented. Your workflows are documented. Your team is trained. And we are still here if something breaks, if you need something new, or if you just want to talk through whether a new AI instrument is worth adopting. Post-launch support is included for 90 days after your instrument ships. After that, support is available at studio rates -- no retainer, no lock-in.

Safe AI adoption framework

Adoption is about people, not technology.

Bringing AI into a small business is not about technology. It is about people. The risk is not that the AI fails. The risk is that the team does not trust it, does not know how to use it, or uses it in ways that create liability. We work to a simple framework.

Boundaries first

Define what AI can touch and what it cannot. Customer data? Financial decisions? Employment decisions? Legal advice? Each gets a clear rule before any instrument is configured.

Human review built in

Every AI output has a human checkpoint before it ships. The instrument drafts. The operator decides. This is permanent system design, not a temporary training period. Pocket Customer shows every call scorecard fully inspectable before it counts.

Team fluency before tool adoption

We train your team before we deploy instruments. A trained team gets value from almost any tool. An untrained team will be paralysed by the best tools on the market.

Start small, ship fast, compound

One workflow. One instrument. One team. Ship it. Measure it. Then expand. Organisational transformation is not a big bang. It is compound tempo.

Custom agents

Instruments built for your operation.

Some businesses need instruments that do not exist in the catalogue. A customer triage system for a specific industry. A compliance checker for a regulated workflow. An internal knowledge agent trained on your SOPs and policy documents.

We build custom agents as part of the implementation sprint. You describe the friction. We design the agent. You test it. We refine it. You own it. Same commercial model: one price, owned forever.

The difference between a custom agent and a PerformOS instrument is scope. Instruments are built for common operational patterns. Custom agents are built for your specific operation. Both ship the same way.
