The 12-week course

Custom learning, sized to your lifestyle.

It starts with a discovery call. From that conversation we build you a complete, fully customised 12-week AI plan. Twenty minutes a day or two hours on Sunday. Audible on the commute or written at your desk. The course shapes itself around how you actually live and how you actually learn. There is no fixed curriculum because there is no fixed learner. Every module, every week, built for you.

Mode 01

Audible.

Lessons you listen to on the commute, at the gym, or while you cook. Mobile-first. No screen required. Learning that fits the day you already have.

Mode 02

Video explainer.

Short visual breakdowns of every concept. Watch once, replay the part that did not click. Pause-friendly, replay-friendly, no rush.

Mode 03

Detailed breakdowns.

Explicit written step-by-step instructions you work through at your desk. Print-friendly. Reference-grade. Mark up as you go.

Output 01 . The playbook

Your AI playbook, yours forever.

Every prompt, every custom agent, every workflow you build across the 12 weeks lands in a playbook you keep. Your reference, in your words, built from your own work. As AI keeps shifting, you already have a guide you trust because you wrote it yourself.

Output 02 . The capstone

A practical capstone, not a quiz.

Week 12 is one real project. Build something AI-powered you actually use, automate something tedious, ship a custom agent, design a workflow that saves you time every week. Competence is proven by what you can build, not by what you can recite.
