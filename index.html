Where AI pays off

Concrete starting points we see working every week.
Admin

Cut hours of admin every week

Use AI to draft, summarise, and tidy. Email replies, meeting notes, proposals, and reports become first drafts in seconds.

Customer

Respond to customers faster

Use AI to triage inbound, draft replies, and answer common questions. Your team handles the same volume in half the time.

Onboarding

Onboard staff in less time

Turn SOPs and tribal knowledge into searchable AI assistants. Faster ramp, fewer interruptions, less reliance on senior staff.

Leads

Recover missed-call revenue

Build a simple AI agent that texts back missed calls, qualifies, and books appointments. Stop losing leads to the competitor who answered first.

Sales

Lift rep confidence and conversion

Use AI roleplay to train staff on objections, scripts, and tone before the real conversation. Reps walk into calls ready, not rusty.

Knowledge

Make internal knowledge searchable

Turn your shared drive into a searchable AI assistant. Staff ask in plain English and get answers, instead of hunting through 200 folders.
